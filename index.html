Sunil Gupta Sir is the Founder, Director & Chief Mentor of Inspiration IAS Academy, and one of India's most respected authorities in UPSC preparation. An alumnus of IIT-BHU and Kurukshetra University, he has dedicated over two decades to making quality guidance accessible to civil services aspirants across the country.

A specialist in General Studies (Polity & Governance), Ethics, and Public Administration, he has shaped thousands of IAS, IPS and IFS officers who today serve the nation. His students have achieved ranks including AIR 1, AIR 3, AIR 5, AIR 6, AIR 7 and hundreds more across UPSC CSE and State PCS examinations.
