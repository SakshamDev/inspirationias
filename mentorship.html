How It Works

6 Structured Stages of Mentorship

A systematically designed, chronological programme that takes you through every phase of your UPSC journey.

1

Personal Interaction with Aspirant

A deep-dive assessment session to understand exactly where you stand.

  • Assessment of current academic preparation level
  • Identification of stress components retarding performance
  • Analysis of weak areas with actionable measures
  • Review of existing schedule and physical capability
2

Personal Flow-Chart Design

Designing your personalised mission roadmap with clarity and direction.

  • Goal clarity with time-bound orientation on a personal level
  • Strategically designed phase-wise programming of the mission
3

Weekly Schedule with Sessions

Breaking down the flow-chart into weekly actionable assignments.

  • Programs divided into weekly assignments based on the flow-chart
  • Tailored-made sessions to accomplish weekly targets
4

Academic Material Support

Comprehensive resources to fuel your preparation at every step.

  • Documentation, videos & digitally interactive mentoring materials
  • Previous Years Questions (PYQs) with point-wise solutions
  • Contemporary information for material updates
5

Feedback & Regulation

Continuous assessment and professional support for course correction.

  • Results assessed with comprehensive analysis
  • Professional assessment to build academic & psychological competence
6

One-to-One Doubt Clearance

Direct personal interaction with your mentor for academic and stress-related guidance.

  • Share academic and preparation doubts with prior information
  • Personalised feedback and direction from the mentor
